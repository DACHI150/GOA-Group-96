About GOA Martial Arts

GOA Martial Arts Academy teaches a variety of martial arts, including mixed martial arts (MMA), kickboxing, jiu-jitsu, and self-defense techniques.
Martial arts are important because they develop physical strength, endurance, discipline, self-confidence, and help people defend themselves.

Training Programs

Striking and Grappling

Striking refers to stand-up combat styles that focus on delivering direct and powerful attacks using punches, kicks, elbows, and knees.
These techniques are used to control distance and dominate opponents.

Grappling is a fundamental category of combat sports and martial arts that focuses on close-quarters techniques.
It includes clinching, takedowns, throws, and ground fighting used to control or submit an opponent.
